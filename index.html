What is Gunpla?

Gunpla is a abreviate terms from "Gundam Plastic Model Kits"

as the full name suggest this product is heavily related to Mobile Suit Gundam franchise, as the design comes directly from the animated series

How to Gunpla?

it's model kit, you uought it then you built it

Gunpla can is categoraized into mainly 4 types or in this case "grade"

Entry Grade(EG)

10$-12$

  1. scale: 1/144
  2. Beginner Friendly
  3. Can be assembled without using any tools
  4. Low part counts
  5. Decent range of articulation
High Grade(HG)

10$-35$

  1. scale: 1/144
  2. everyboy can enjoy building model kit in this grade
  3. has the widest category to choose from
  4. easily to customized due to universal point/part within or across toy line
